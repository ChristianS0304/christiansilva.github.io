Disney World

The holy grail of Florida theme parks, where magical memories are made. Disney World has a surplus of attractions to offer for any age! Disney World has Epcot, Magic Kindgon, Animal Kingdom, and Hollywood Studios.

Disney World allows you to explore the film, animal, and magical aspects of Disney. Hollywood Studios gives you a chance to see Star Wars characters, the Tower of Terror, and ride the Rock 'n' Roller Coaster Starring Aerosmith.

Magic Kingdom

Explore Cinderella's castle, meet everyone from Mickey to the princesses and much more! Magic Kindgom also has a nightly fireworks show that is absolutely spectacular to watch. Get on rides such as Space Mountain, the Seven Dwarfs Mine Train, and the Barnstormer.

Busch Gardens

This is the place to go if you are seeking fast packed thrilled rides. There are also animal experiences and seasonal events for the whole family, as well as not so nerve-racking roller coasters for all ages.

Primary location located in Tampa Bay, Busch Gardens provides the best thrill rides in Florida. Enjoy rides such as Tigris, Cobra's Curse, Falcon's Fury, and the new 2020 Hybrid Coaster coming soon! Apart from rides, Busch Gardens has many animals that you can meet, adding for the opportunity to take breathtaking pictures with wild animals and creatures.

The Rides

Tigris: Florida's tallest Launch Coaster, opening April 19, 2019

Cobra's Curse: A family spin coaster, a Florida first

2020 Hybrid Coaster: Constructing underway for a New Hybrid Coaster, coming 2020

Falcon's Fury: A massive 335 foot tower drop

Universal Studios

Wondered what it's like to feel like visiting the shops of Hogsmeade? Or even exploring the mysteries of the Hogwarts castle in your house's robes? Universal Studios has that and much more to offer!

Rides include Revenge of the Mummy, The Simpsons Ride, Harry Potter and the Escape from Gringotts, and Hollywood Ride Rockit. During Halloween, there is also Halloween Horror Nights, where every direction you look can be spooky if you'd like some adrenaline pumping action.

Wizarding World of Harry Potter

Feel like a wizard by exploring the Wizarding World of Harry Potter while having a butter beer with your friends and family. Have a chance to enjoy all the delicious candy and food that you could only get in this magical world.
